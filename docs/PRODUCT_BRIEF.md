# Product Brief — Portfolio V2

## Owner
**Muhammad Juan Al Firdaus**
Frontend Developer (React / Next.js) · 5+ years experience · Jakarta, Indonesia

## Goal
Increase job opportunities at remote global companies by showcasing technical depth, real-world impact, and engineering thinking through a modern, memorable portfolio website.

## Target Users
1. **Tech recruiters** — scanning for senior frontend candidates
2. **CTOs / Hiring managers** — evaluating technical depth and reliability
3. **Startup founders** — looking for senior frontend hires who can own features

## Positioning Statement
> "Frontend engineer who builds complex SaaS platforms that thousands of people actually use."

## Core Desired Perception
**Reliable.** When someone visits this site, they should feel:
- "This person is senior and trustworthy"
- "This person ships real products at scale"
- "This person thinks like an engineer, not just a coder"

## Positioning Pillars
1. **LMS/EdTech Domain Specialist** — Deep experience in learning platforms with multi-role systems
2. **Scale-Proven** — Built frontends serving 7,000+ users across education and enterprise
3. **Systems Thinker** — Reusable component architectures, 25% faster dev cycles
4. **Cross-Cultural Remote Ready** — Worked with Australian, Indonesian, and distributed teams

## What This Portfolio Is NOT
- Not a generic template
- Not "Creative Front End Developer" branding
- No over-animated gimmicks
- No empty "innovation" or "passion" claims
- No project cards with just screenshots and tech badges

## Key Differentiator
Full code-generated 3D interactive experience using React Three Fiber + GLSL shaders. No external 3D assets — everything built in code. This itself demonstrates WebGL/3D capability and engineering depth.

## Success Metrics
- Recruiter spends > 60 seconds on site
- At least 1 case study read per visit
- Resume downloaded or contact initiated
- Portfolio itself becomes a conversation starter in interviews
